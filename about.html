About TOLOMAKAN!

Hello there! This is actually a Client side html for our TOLO-Makan API

Let us ask you some important questions before you continue to read on :

1) Have you felt like deciding what to eat for your meals is a chore?
2) Have you also felt like you really don't know what food are there around you or at the place you are at?
3) Have you also felt like you need an app that helps you search for a food that is wallet-friendly

Thus, to meet those "stringent" requirement, we decide to create an app to solve the dreadful problem of having to think of what food should we eat today!
